About Us

Life is a roller coaster of situations and our reactions to them, but one thing that stays constant in life is food. We all can be divided on various factors, but what unites all of us together is the irreplaceable love for food. The one thing that irritates us about having a good meal is the long, endless queues resulting in frustration and hence loss in appetite. Especially at the professional institutions when the break is very limited and the queues seem endless. We at Snatched , provide a thoughtful solution to this issue. Through our application, you can order your favorite meal by a touch and we will handle all the chaos. What you get is a divine, hustle-free takeaway meal and all that is left to do is enjoy the Food. Isn’t it simply awesome? Don’t waste your precious lunch breaks in the long queues and spoil your mood. Our team will make sure that you get your instant meal for an instant hunger and an instant elevation in happiness.
